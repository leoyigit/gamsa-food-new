What is juk?

Korean juk (죽) is a rice porridge that has comforted generations — similar to Chinese congee, Filipino arroz caldo, and Thai jok. Enjoyed for breakfast, as a soothing meal when you're under the weather, or any time of day.

Traditionally it takes hours — soaking rice, chopping, slow-cooking. We handle the prep so you get the bowl in minutes.
