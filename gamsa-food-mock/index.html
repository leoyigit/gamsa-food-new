What is juk?

Korean juk (죽) is a rice porridge that has comforted generations — cousin to Chinese congee, Filipino arroz caldo, and Thai jok. Enjoyed for breakfast, when you're under the weather, or any time you need something soothing.

Traditionally it takes hours: soaking rice, chopping, slow-cooking. We do the work, so you get the bowl in two and a half minutes.
